We have been offering gymnastics classes for gymnasts ages 2-18 since 1981.
We also offer special programs for both current students and non-members.

Our Classes:

  • 2 Year-Old
  • Preschool
  • Kinder
  • Girls
  • Boys

The 2 Year-Old "Gym Rompers" class is a modified preschool program for two year-olds.

  • Gymnastics apparatus, mats, low beams, and small hand apparatus are used in conjunction with individual and group activities.
  • The children acquire a social education as they follow directions, wait their turn, and interact with their peers.
  • A parent must assist in the "Gym Romper" class.

More information & class times >

Our Preschool "Tiny Tumblers" class is a multi-educational preschool movement class. Each week is a new learning experience.

  • Gymnastics apparatus, mats, low beams, and small hand apparatus are used in conjunction with individual and group activities.
  • The children acquire a social education as they follow directions, wait their turn, and interact with their peers.

More information & class times >

Our Kinder program is designed as a bridge program between our "Tiny Tumbler" program and our girls and boys program.

  • Kinder students still work on motor skill development but also progress through more gymnastics skills.
  • Gymnastics apparatus, mats, low beams, and small hand apparatus are used in conjunction with individual and group activities.
